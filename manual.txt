*****HOW TO PLAY*****
Movement: LEFT and RIGHT ARROW KEYS
Jump: SPACE
Shoot: C
Change Bullet Color: SHIFT (LEFT, RIGHT)
Drain Color from Enemy: X
Pause: ESC or ENTER

*****GOALS*****
The object of the game is for the character to restore color to the world. 
The character obtains ammo by DRAINING enemy color, thus killing them. 
They also solve puzzles by SHOOTING the color they obtained. 
Use different colors by pressing SHIFT to solve the puzzle correctly.
